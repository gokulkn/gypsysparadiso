5 Best Places to Visit

01. The Hidden Gem

Chilanthiyar Falls

A secret waterfall hidden deep in the forest.

Far from the tourist crowds, this pristine waterfall requires a short trek through the jungle. It is one of the few places where you can enjoy the water in complete privacy.

Distance from Gypsys Paradiso: 20 mins Trek.

02. Seasonal Delight

Strawberry Farms

The 'Winter Vegetable Capital' of Kerala.

Vattavada is famous for its cool climate crops. Walk through lush strawberry fields, pluck fresh berries, and experience the rural farm life. Best season: Nov - Feb.

Distance from Gypsys Paradiso: 15 mins Drive.

03. The Viewpoint

Top Station

Where the clouds meet the mountains.

Located on the border of Kerala and Tamil Nadu, this historic viewpoint offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats. It is the highest point in Munnar.

Distance from Gypsys Paradiso: 45 mins Drive.

04. The Wild

Pampadum Shola

The smallest national park in Kerala.

Drive through the dense, mist-covered shola forests. Keep an eye out for the rare Nilgiri Marten and elephants. The road itself is the destination here.

Distance from Gypsys Paradiso: On the way.

Perfect One Day Trip Plan

09:00 AM: Reach Top Station for the morning mist view.

10:30 AM: Drive through Pampadum Shola Forest (Animal spotting).

11:30 AM: Visit Vattavada Strawberry & Vegetable Farms.

01:00 PM: Lunch at Vattavada Village.

02:30 PM: Trek to Chilanthiyar Falls (Hidden Gem).

05:00 PM: Check-in at Gypsys Paradiso for the sunset campfire.
