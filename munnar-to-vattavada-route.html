The Legend

Vattavada to Kodaikanal

Many travelers ask about the historic route connecting Vattavada to Kodaikanal. This ancient trade route, also known as the "Escape Road," is currently a popular trekking trail.

  • 👣 Distance: Approx 20-25 KM trek.
  • 🌲 Terrain: Dense Shola forest & Grasslands.
  • ⚠️ Status: Forest permission required. Not a motorable pleasant drive yet.

Tip: Stay at Gypsys Paradiso as your basecamp before starting this legendary trek.
