Light Up
The Night.

← Back to all stories

The fire is crackling, the stars are out, and you are huddled together in blankets. A bonfire night at Gypsys Paradiso is magical on its own, but a few games can turn it into a memory you'll laugh about for years. Here are our top 5 picks.

1. Two Truths and a Lie

A classic icebreaker. Each person says three statements about themselves—two true and one false. The group has to guess which one is the lie. You'll be surprised at the secrets that come out!

2. The Ghost Story Relay

One person starts a spooky story with a single sentence. The next person adds another sentence, and so on. The goal is to make it as terrifying (or ridiculous) as possible. In the dark forest setting of Vattavada, this hits different.

3. Antakshari

No Indian gathering is complete without it. Split into two teams and sing your hearts out. Don't worry about being in tune; the louder, the better.

4. Dumb Charades

Use the firelight to your advantage. The flickering shadows make acting out movie titles even more dramatic and hilarious.

5. Mafia / Werewolf

If you have a large group, this social deduction game is intense. Assign roles (Villagers, Mafia, Doctor, Detective) and try to survive the "night." It gets very competitive!

We Provide the Fire

At Gypsys Paradiso, every group gets their own private campfire. We provide the wood and the space; you bring the energy.

So put away your phones, gather around the warmth, and let the games begin.
