Paws in the Hills:
Pet Friendly Guide.

Your dog deserves a vacation too. But the forest has rules.

Vattavada is one of the most pet-friendly destinations because of the open space. Gypsys Paradiso and several other campsites welcome pets.

Safety First

  • Ticks & Leeches: The Shola grass is full of them. Apply pet-safe tick repellent before you arrive. Check their paws daily.
  • Wild Animals: Do not let your dog off-lease at night. Bison and leopards are real threats.
  • Anxiety: It gets very dark and quiet. Some city dogs get anxious. Bring their favorite blanket.

Where to Walk?

The vegetable farm trails are perfect for long walks. The soil is soft on their paws, and the open visibility makes it safe.
