Tale of Two
Villages.

← Back to all stories

Kanthalloor and Vattavada are often called the "Kashmir of Kerala" because of their ability to grow winter fruits like apples and strawberries. They are located near each other, but offer very different vibes.

The Vibe

Kanthalloor is more developed for tourism. It has more resorts, homestays, and paved roads. It feels like a small hill station town.

Vattavada is rawer and more secluded. It is surrounded by protected Shola forests and national parks. It feels like a hidden village stuck in time.

Which One Should You Choose?

Choose Kanthalloor if:

You want a comfortable stay in a resort, easy access to shops, and are traveling with elderly people who prefer less travel.

Choose Vattavada if:

You want adventure, privacy, and a true nature experience. If you want to trek, camp, and see wildlife, Vattavada is the clear winner.

Why Not Both?

They are only about 40km apart. You can stay in Vattavada and take a day trip to Kanthalloor to buy apples and visit the Muniyaras (Dolmens).

Both villages are gems of Idukki. You can't go wrong with either.
