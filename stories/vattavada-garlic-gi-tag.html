The White Gold:
GI Tagged Garlic.

It is not just garlic. It is medicine.

Officially known as 'Kanthalloor-Vattavada Veluthulli', this specific variety was granted the Geographical Indication (GI) tag in 2022. But why?

The Science

Grown in rich, sulfur-heavy soils at 6000+ ft, this garlic has:

  • Higher Sulphide Content: Makes it more pungent and medicinally potent.
  • Long Shelf Life: Can be stored for up to a year without drying out.
  • High Allicin: The compound responsible for fighting cholesterol and boosting immunity.

Vattavada vs Others

Unlike the large, white, water-filled garlic you get in supermarkets (often from China or North India), Vattavada garlic is smaller, has a purple tinge, and is packed with oil. You only need one clove where you would usually use three.

It is traditionally smoked-dried largely by the farmers to preserve it.
