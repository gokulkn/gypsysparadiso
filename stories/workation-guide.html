Why stare at a blank wall when you can stare at a waterfall? The concept of 'Workation' (Work + Vacation) is booming, and Vattavada is quickly becoming a favorite spot for digital nomads.

Connectivity

The biggest concern for remote workers is internet. In Vattavada, Jio and BSNL 4G work reasonably well. We also provide dedicated WiFi at our reception area for those important video calls.

Power Backup

Power cuts can happen in the hills, especially during monsoons. At Gypsys Paradiso, we have generator backup to ensure your laptop never runs out of juice.

Daily Routine

Imagine this: Wake up to birdsong. Have a fresh strawberry smoothie for breakfast. Work for 4 hours. Take a lunch break and walk through a vegetable farm. Finish work by evening and join the campfire. It beats the city traffic any day.

Long Stay Packages

We offer special rates for stays longer than a week. This includes all meals (home-cooked and healthy), laundry service, and housekeeping.

Digital Detox Hours

We encourage our guests to disconnect after work hours. No emails after 7 PM. Just you, the fire, and the stars.

Your best work happens when you are at peace. Come find your peace here.
