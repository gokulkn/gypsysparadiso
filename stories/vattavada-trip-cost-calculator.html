Trip Cost:
Real Numbers.

Planning a budget? Here is the real breakdown of costs for a standard 2-Day trip.

1. Transport (The Big One)

  • Getting to Vattavada (Fuel): From Kochi (Round trip ~350km): Approx ₹3,000 - ₹4,000 in fuel for a car.
  • Jeep Rental (Local): To reach deep campsites or farms, you need a 4x4 Jeep.
    Cost: ₹2,500 - ₹3,500 per off-road trip (holds 6-7 people).
  • Bus: KSRTC from Munnar to Vattavada cost is under ₹50 (very cheap but infrequent).

2. Accommodation

  • Budget Tent Camping: ₹1,200 - ₹1,500 per person (usually includes dinner/breakfast).
  • Glamping / Luxury Tents: ₹2,500 - ₹4,000 per person (includes ensuite bathrooms, BBQ, private campfire).
  • Homestays: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 per room (basic facilities).

3. Food & Extras

  • Meals: Minimal cost. A good Kerala meal in the village is ₹100-₹150.
  • Strawberry Picking: You pay for what you pick. Approx ₹400-₹600 per kg.
  • Forest Entry: ~₹100 per person/vehicle total.

Total Estimate (Per Person - Group of 4)

Budget Backpacker: ₹2,500 total (Bus + Budget Camp + Basic Food).
Comfort Traveler: ₹5,500 total (Car + Jeep Safari + Glamping + BBQ).
