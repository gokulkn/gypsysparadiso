Traveling with kids can be stressful. You worry about safety, food, and keeping them entertained. Vattavada ticks all the boxes for a perfect, stress-free family vacation.

Is Camping Safe for Kids?

This is the #1 question we get. At Gypsys Paradiso, safety is our priority. Our campsite is fenced, well-lit, and staffed 24/7. We have hosted families with infants as young as 6 months old.

Why Kids Love It

Forget iPads and video games. Here, nature is the playground.

  • Farm Visits: Kids love seeing where their food comes from. Let them pull a carrot out of the ground or pick a strawberry.
  • Easy Treks: The Pine Forest walk is flat and easy, perfect for little legs.
  • Campfire Stories: Sitting around a fire roasting marshmallows is a core memory in the making.

Parent Hacks for Vattavada

1. Pack Layers

It gets cold at night. Dress your kids in layers so they can peel them off as the sun comes up.

2. Bring Snacks

While we provide food, kids can be picky. Bring their favorite biscuits or chips for the drive.

3. Book a Cabin

If you are worried about sleeping in a tent, book one of our luxury cabins. You get the camping vibe with solid walls and an attached bathroom.

Digital Detox

Use this trip to disconnect. Teach your kids how to identify birds, how to spot constellations, or just how to enjoy silence.

Give your children the gift of the outdoors. They might get a little muddy, but they will be happier for it.
