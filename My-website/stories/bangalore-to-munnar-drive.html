Bangalore traffic can be soul-crushing. The antidote? A 475km drive to the cool, misty hills of Munnar. It's a long drive, but the changing landscapes make it worth every mile.

The Best Route

Bangalore → Hosur → Salem → Perundurai → Udumalpet → Marayoor → Munnar → Vattavada

This route is preferred over the Mysore route because the roads are wider and faster.

Itinerary & Pitstops

05:00 AM

Start Early

Leave Bangalore before sunrise to beat the Electronic City traffic. Aim to cross Hosur by 6 AM.

08:30 AM

Breakfast at Salem

Stop at one of the many A2B or Saravana Bhavan outlets on the highway for a solid South Indian breakfast.

01:00 PM

Lunch at Udumalpet

This is the last major town before the ghat section begins. Fuel up your car and your stomach.

03:00 PM

Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

As you cross into Kerala, you enter the Chinnar forest. Drive slow—elephants and peacocks are common sightings here.

04:30 PM

Marayoor Sandalwood Forest

Drive through the only natural sandalwood forest in Kerala. The smell in the air is unmistakable.

Forest Checkposts

There are multiple checkposts after Udumalpet. Keep your vehicle documents handy and avoid carrying plastic bags or alcohol, as checking can be strict.

By evening, you'll be sipping tea in Vattavada, far away from the city noise.
