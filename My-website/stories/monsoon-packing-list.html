The monsoon in Vattavada (June to August) is not for the faint-hearted. It rains heavily, the mist is thick, and the leeches are out. But it's also the most beautiful time to visit if you are prepared.

Clothing Tips

Avoid cotton as it takes forever to dry. Stick to quick-dry synthetic fabrics (polyester/nylon). Bring extra pairs of socks—you will need them.

Why Visit in Monsoon?

The waterfalls are at their full glory. The hills are a vibrant green. And there are almost no tourists. It's just you and the raw power of nature.

Pack smart, stay dry, and embrace the rain.
