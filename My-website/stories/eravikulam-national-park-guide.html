The Tahr.

← Back to all stories

Eravikulam National Park (also known as Rajamala) is the most visited attraction in Munnar. It is the sanctuary of the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, a mountain goat found only in these hills.

Getting There from Vattavada

The park entrance is about 45km from Vattavada. It takes roughly 1.5 hours to drive there. We recommend leaving Vattavada by 7:00 AM to reach the park when it opens.

The Safari Experience

Private vehicles are not allowed inside the park. You have to park your car at the entrance and take a forest department bus. The bus ride itself is thrilling, taking you up steep hills with breathtaking views.

Seeing the Nilgiri Tahr

Once the bus drops you off, you can walk on a paved path for about 1km. The Tahrs are very friendly and often come right up to the path. It's a great opportunity for photography.

Important Info

  • Timings: 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM.
  • Tickets: It is highly recommended to book tickets online in advance, especially during weekends and holidays.
  • Closure: The park is closed for the calving season (usually February to March). Check the official website before planning.

Pro Tip

Don't carry plastic bottles or food items inside. The security check is very strict. Enjoy the nature walk and the fresh air.

A visit to Eravikulam is not just about seeing an animal; it's about witnessing the conservation of a species.
