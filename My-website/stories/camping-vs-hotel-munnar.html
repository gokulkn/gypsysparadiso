Walls vs.
Canvas.

← Back to all stories

When planning a trip to Munnar, the default choice is often a hotel. Four walls, a TV, and a buffet breakfast. It's comfortable, sure. But is it memorable? We believe that to truly experience the mountains, you need to be in them, not just looking at them through a window.

Enter Glamping (Glamorous Camping)—the perfect middle ground between roughing it out in a sleeping bag and being trapped in a concrete box.

The Comparison

Feature Standard Hotel Glamping at Gypsys
Connection to Nature View from a window Immersive (Sounds, Smells, Air)
Privacy Shared corridors/walls Private standalone tents/cabins
Atmosphere Generic & Commercial Rustic & Romantic
Activities TV & WiFi Campfire, Trekking, Stargazing

1. Waking Up to Birdsong

In a hotel, you wake up to an alarm or the sound of traffic. In a glamping tent, the canvas walls let in the sounds of the forest. You wake up naturally as the sun hits your tent, listening to birds you've never heard before.

2. The Campfire Factor

Most hotels don't allow fires. At Gypsys Paradiso, a private campfire is part of the package. There is something primal and deeply relaxing about sitting around a fire, roasting marshmallows (or grilling chicken), and talking late into the night under a blanket of stars.

3. Luxury without Compromise

Many people fear camping because of the lack of amenities. Glamping solves this. Our tents come with comfortable beds, warm duvets, electricity, and clean restrooms. You get the adventure of camping with the comfort of a hotel room.

Digital Detox

While we do have connectivity, the environment encourages you to put the phone down. It's about reconnecting with your partner, your friends, or just yourself.

So next time, skip the concrete. Come sleep under the stars.
