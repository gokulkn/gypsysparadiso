There are hundreds of campsites in Munnar. So why do travelers keep choosing Gypsys Paradiso? It's simple: We don't just sell a place to sleep; we sell an experience.

1. Privacy Like No Other

Most campsites are crowded fields where tents are pitched inches apart. At Gypsys Paradiso, we are located on a private hillside in Vattavada. You get space, silence, and seclusion.

2. The Food

Forget instant noodles. Our local chefs prepare authentic Kerala cuisine using vegetables plucked fresh from the neighboring farms. Our BBQ nights are legendary.

3. Safety First

We are one of the few campsites that is completely fenced and has 24/7 security. This makes us the top choice for families and solo female travelers.

4. Unique Activities

From trekking to the secret Chilanthiyar waterfalls to off-road jeep safaris and strawberry picking, we curate activities that you won't find on a standard tour package.

5. The Vibe

We are not a hotel. We are a community. Our campfires are places where strangers become friends, stories are shared, and memories are made.

Come Home

We invite you to leave the chaos of the city behind and find your sanctuary in the hills. Welcome to Gypsys Paradiso.
