Macro World:
Tiny Wonders.

Put down the wide angle. The real magic is in the details.

The Shola forests are a humid, moss-covered ecosystem. For a macro photographer, it is a treasure chest.

What to Shoot?

  • Rare Orchids: Look for tiny epiphytic orchids growing on old tree trunks.
  • Moss & Ferns: The forest floor is a carpet of neon green moss with alien-like sporophytes.
  • Frogs & Insects: The damp environment hosts colorful bush frogs and iridescent beetles.

Best Spots

The trekking path inside Pampadum Shola National Park. The light filtering through the canopy creates a natural soft-box effect.
