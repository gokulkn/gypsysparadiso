The journey from the coastal heat of Kochi to the misty hills of Vattavada is a transition through three different climates. It's a 170km drive that takes about 5-6 hours, but you'll want to take all day.

Best Pitstops

Stop 1

Cheeyappara Waterfalls

Located between Neriamangalam and Adimali. It's a seven-step waterfall right by the roadside. Perfect for a quick photo and a hot tea.

Stop 2

Valara Waterfalls

Just a few kilometers after Cheeyappara. It's surrounded by thick forests and is a great spot to stretch your legs.

Stop 3

Gap Road Viewpoint

After crossing Munnar town towards Vattavada, you hit the famous Gap Road. The views of the valley here are breathtaking. On a clear day, you can see all the way to Tamil Nadu.

Stop 4

Pampadum Shola National Park

The final stretch to Vattavada passes through this reserve forest. Drive slow, roll down your windows, and look out for the Nilgiri Langur and Gaur (Indian Bison).

Driving Tip

The road from Munnar to Vattavada is narrow and winding. Honk at curves and give way to uphill traffic. If you are not comfortable driving, we can arrange a pickup from Munnar town.

The destination is beautiful, but this drive is an experience in itself.
