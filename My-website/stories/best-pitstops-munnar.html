The drive to Munnar is famous for a reason. It's not just a road; it's a destination. Here are the top 5 places you absolutely must stop at on your way up.

1. Cheeyappara Waterfalls

You can't miss this. It's right on the side of the NH 85 highway. The water cascades down seven rocky steps. It's a great spot for a quick selfie and some roasted corn from street vendors.

2. Valara Waterfalls

Located just 10km from Cheeyappara, this waterfall is surrounded by thick jungle. You can't go down to the water, but the view from the road is spectacular.

3. Karadipara Viewpoint

A hidden gem before you reach Munnar town. It offers a panoramic view of the valley and the winding roads you just climbed. Perfect for a sunset stop.

4. Spice Gardens

There are several spice gardens near Adimali. Take a guided tour to see cardamom, pepper, vanilla, and cocoa plants. It's educational and smells amazing.

5. Roadside Tea Shops

Nothing beats a hot cup of 'Kattan Chaya' (Black Tea) and a 'Pazhampori' (Banana Fritter) in the misty weather. Stop at any small shack with a view; the tea always tastes better in the hills.

Pro Tip

Keep your camera ready but be careful of the monkeys at these stops. They are cute but can snatch food from your hands!

Take your time. The mountains aren't going anywhere.
