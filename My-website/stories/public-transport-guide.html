Exploring Vattavada doesn't have to be expensive. The KSRTC bus ride from Munnar is not only the cheapest way to get here (approx. ₹60), but it also offers some of the best window-seat views in Kerala.

Where to Catch the Bus

Buses to Vattavada start from the Munnar KSRTC Bus Stand in the main town. It's a small but busy station.

Bus Timings (Munnar to Vattavada)

Note: Timings are subject to change. It's always best to reach the station 15 minutes early.

The Journey

The 45km journey takes about 1.5 hours. The bus passes through Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Top Station. Sit on the right side for the best views of the valley.

Alternative: Shared Jeeps

If you miss the bus, you can take a shared jeep from the Munnar Jeep Stand. They are faster but cost a bit more (approx. ₹100-₹150 per seat).

Reaching Gypsys Paradiso

Get down at the Vattavada Bus Stand. From there, you can hire a local jeep to reach our campsite, or call us for a pickup arrangement.

Traveling by bus is the most authentic way to experience the local culture. You'll share space with farmers carrying fresh vegetables and students returning from school.
