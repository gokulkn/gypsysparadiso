The Checkpost:
6 PM Deadline.

The road to Vattavada is not open 24/7. It passes through a protected National Park.

To reach Vattavada, you must drive through the Pampadum Shola National Park. The Forest Department strictly enforces entry timings to protect wildlife.

Timings

OPEN: 6:00 AM
CLOSE: 6:00 PM

If you reach the checkpost at Top Station after 6:00 PM, you will not be allowed to enter. There are no exceptions. You will have to turn back and sleep in Munnar.

Entry Fees

You need to purchase a pass at the checkpost. Keep cash ready.

  • Vehicle Fee: Approx ₹50-100 depending on vehicle type (Car/Bike).
  • Passenger Fee: Nominal fee per person.
  • Camera Fee: Extra charge for professional cameras.

Forest Rules

  • No Stopping: You cannot stop your vehicle inside the Shola forest stretch except at designated viewpoints.
  • No Plastic: Plastics are strictly banned. Do not throw bottles.
  • No Horn: Honking disturbs the elephants and bison often found on this road.
  • Speed Limit: Drive slow. Wildlife crossing is frequent.
