For travelers coming from Tamil Nadu, the drive from Madurai to Vattavada is a visual treat. Unlike the Kochi route, this one offers dramatic views of the Tamil Nadu plains as you climb up the Western Ghats.

Distance: Approx. 140 km | Time: 4-5 hours

Why Choose This Route?

It's shorter and less crowded than the Munnar town route. You bypass the heavy traffic of Munnar and enter Vattavada directly from the eastern side.

Highlights Along the Way

Stop 1

Theni

A bustling town at the foothills. Great place to stock up on snacks or have a meal before the ghat section starts.

Stop 2

Bodimettu Viewpoint

As you climb the hairpin bends from Bodi to Bodimettu, the view of the sprawling plains below is spectacular, especially at sunrise or sunset.

Stop 3

Cardamom Hills

After crossing the state border into Kerala, the landscape changes to lush green cardamom plantations. The air gets cooler and fresher.

Stop 4

Poopara

From Poopara, you take the turn towards Vattavada. This stretch is incredibly scenic, passing through the Shola National Parks.

Border Crossing

You will cross the Tamil Nadu - Kerala checkpost at Bodimettu. Ensure you have your vehicle's original documents and pollution certificate, as checks are frequent.

This route is a favorite among bikers for its smooth roads and challenging curves.
