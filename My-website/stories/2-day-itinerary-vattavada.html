48 Hours in
Paradise.

← Back to all stories

Two days might not seem like enough, but if you plan it right, you can experience the essence of Vattavada without rushing. Here is our curated itinerary for a perfect weekend getaway.

Day 1: Arrival & Exploration

12:00 PM - Check-in at Gypsys Paradiso

Arrive at the campsite. Freshen up in your luxury tent and enjoy a welcome drink made from local passion fruit.

02:00 PM - Strawberry Farm Visit

Take a short drive to the strawberry farms. Walk through the rows, pick your own fruit, and buy some fresh jam to take home.

04:30 PM - Sunset at Pazhathottam

Head to the Pazhathottam View Point. Watch the sun dip below the mountains, painting the sky in shades of orange and purple.

07:30 PM - Campfire & BBQ

Return to the campsite. Gather around your private bonfire, grill some marinated chicken or paneer, and enjoy the starry night.

Day 2: Adventure & Departure

06:00 AM - Morning Trek

Wake up early for a guided trek to the Pine Forest or Chilanthiyar Falls. The morning mist makes the forest look magical.

09:00 AM - Breakfast with a View

Enjoy a hot breakfast of Idli and Sambhar at the campsite, overlooking the valley.

11:00 AM - Visit Koviloor Village

Before you leave, stop by the ancient village of Koviloor. See the traditional houses and buy some winter vegetables from the market.

Pro Tip

Download offline maps before you arrive, as network coverage can be spotty in some areas. And don't forget to pack warm clothes for the night!

This itinerary gives you a mix of relaxation, adventure, and culture. Ready to pack your bags?
