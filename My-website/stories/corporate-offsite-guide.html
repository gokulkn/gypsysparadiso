Is your team burned out? Are Zoom calls replacing real connection? It's time to take the office to the mountains. Vattavada offers the perfect environment to recharge, reconnect, and rebuild team spirit.

Why Vattavada for Offsites?

Unlike typical resorts in Munnar where teams get stuck in conference rooms, Vattavada forces you to step outside. The lack of distractions and the immersive nature experience fosters genuine bonding.

Team Building Activities

1. The Trek Challenge

Nothing builds camaraderie like conquering a peak together. Our guided treks to Chilanthiyar Falls or the Pine Forest require teamwork and encouragement.

2. Outdoor Cooking

Split into teams and cook a meal over an open fire. It's a fun, chaotic, and delicious way to test collaboration skills.

3. Bonfire Brainstorming

Ditch the whiteboard. Some of the best ideas happen around a campfire, under the stars, with a hot drink in hand.

Capacity & Facilities

Gypsys Paradiso can host teams of up to 30 people. We provide high-speed Wi-Fi (if you really need it), a projector for presentations, and customized meal plans.

Sample Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival, Ice-breaker games, Sunset hike, BBQ Dinner.
  • Day 2: Morning Yoga, Team Trek, Strategy Session, Gala Dinner with Music.
  • Day 3: Farm Visit & Departure.

Invest in your team's well-being. A trip to Vattavada is more than just a holiday; it's a reset button.
