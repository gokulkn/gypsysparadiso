Your honeymoon should be memorable, not generic. Instead of the usual hotel room with a buffet breakfast, imagine waking up in a luxury tent, surrounded by mist, with only the birds for company.

Why Glamping for Honeymoon?

Glamping (Glamorous Camping) offers the perfect balance of adventure and comfort. You get the privacy of a secluded location without sacrificing amenities like a comfortable bed and a clean restroom.

Romantic Experiences in Vattavada

1. Private Candlelight Dinner

Forget crowded restaurants. At Gypsys Paradiso, we set up a private table for you right outside your tent, under the open sky. Enjoy a meal by the warmth of your own campfire.

2. Strawberry Picking Date

Walk hand-in-hand through the strawberry farms. It's a sweet and fun activity that makes for adorable photos.

3. Sunset at a Secret Spot

We can guide you to a hidden viewpoint near the campsite where you can watch the sunset in complete privacy, away from the tourists.

The "Us" Time

The best part about Vattavada is the silence. There is no traffic, no noise, and no distractions. It's just you, your partner, and the mountains. It's the perfect way to start your new life together.
