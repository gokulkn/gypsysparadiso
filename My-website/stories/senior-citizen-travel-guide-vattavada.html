Seniors Guide:
Comfort & Care.

Adventure is ageless, but comfort is necessary.

Many seniors love Vattavada for its fresh air and silence. Here is how to make the trip comfortable for parents or grandparents.

The Drive

The road is winding. Motion sickness is common.
Tip: Stop at Top Station for tea. Keep windows open for fresh air. Drive slow.

Accessibility

Avoid Tent Camping. Crawling into a ground tent is hard on the knees.
Choose Cabins: Opt for elevated cottages or cabins (like our Mist Cabins) which have proper beds, attached western toilets, and sit-outs with views.

Medical Note

The air is thin and cold. Seniors with asthma should carry inhalers. Keep warm clothing handy at all times.
