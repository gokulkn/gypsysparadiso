Why Vattavada?

Unlike crowded tourist spots, Vattavada offers privacy. At Gypsys Paradiso, you get your own private corner of the forest. No noisy neighbors, no strict curfews (within reason), and total freedom.

The Itinerary

Day 1: The Arrival

Reach by noon. Start with an off-road jeep safari to the campsite. It sets the mood right away. Spend the evening with a private BBQ and campfire. We provide the marinated chicken; you provide the playlist.

Day 2: The Adventure

Wake up late. Head out for a trek to the secret waterfalls. It's a moderate trek, perfect for working up an appetite. In the evening, chill by the stream or play cards in the large community tent.

What We Provide

  • Private Tents: Spacious tents that can fit your whole crew.
  • BBQ Setup: Grills, coal, and marination.
  • Music: We have portable speakers, but feel free to bring your own.
  • Food: Unlimited local Kerala food to keep you fueled.

Responsible Fun

While we want you to have fun, we respect the forest. Loud music is allowed only until 10 PM. After that, it's acoustic vibes only. Also, no littering in the woods.
