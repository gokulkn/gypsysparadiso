The air in Vattavada is different. It's cleaner, thinner, and carries the scent of eucalyptus. It is the perfect environment to reset your nervous system.

Why Practice Here?

Yoga is about connecting with nature. In the city, you practice in a studio. Here, your studio is a hilltop overlooking a valley of clouds. The silence here is not empty; it's healing.

Best Spots for Meditation

1. The Sunrise Rock

Located just a short walk from our campsite. Sit here at 6 AM. As the sun warms your face, you'll feel a sense of calm that no app can give you.

2. Chilanthiyar Stream

The sound of flowing water is a natural white noise. Meditating by the stream helps wash away stress and anxiety.

3. The Pine Forest

The tall pine trees create a natural cathedral. The filtered light and the soft bed of pine needles make it an ideal spot for grounding exercises.

Wellness at Gypsys Paradiso

We offer yoga mats on request. We can also arrange for a local yoga instructor for group sessions if booked in advance. Our food menu includes healthy, organic options to support your detox.

Digital Detox

Wellness isn't just about moving your body; it's about resting your mind. We highly recommend turning off your phone notifications during your stay.

Come for the view, stay for the peace of mind.
