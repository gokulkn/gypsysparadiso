Kerala is generally safe for women, and Vattavada, being a close-knit village community, is even safer. We see many solo female travelers finding solace and adventure here.

Is it Safe?

Yes. The locals are respectful and helpful. Unlike busy tourist hubs where touts can be annoying, people here mind their own business. Crime rates are virtually non-existent.

Accommodation at Gypsys Paradiso

We prioritize the safety of our female guests.

  • Secure Tents: Our tents have proper locks and are pitched in well-lit areas.
  • 24/7 Staff: Our caretaker is available round the clock.
  • Female Friendly: We have clean, private restrooms and changing areas.

Travel Tips

  • Transport: If you are taking the bus, the KSRTC service from Munnar to Vattavada is safe and reliable.
  • Connectivity: Share your live location with a friend or family member. Jio network is good here.
  • Dress Code: While there are no strict rules, it's a rural area, so comfortable trekking wear is best.

Community

You are never truly alone here. Join our nightly campfire. You'll meet other travelers, couples, and families. It's a great way to make friends while maintaining your independence.

Don't wait for company. The best company you can have is yourself.
